Executive Summary

Nigeria's security landscape remains challenging with persistent terrorism, kidnapping, and banditry across all zones. Key developments include ONSA's successful arrest of two Ansaru leaders in August, disrupting major kidnapping networks, while ISWAP advanced tactics with drone usage. Farmer-herder violence surged 64% year-on-year, with over 2 million IDPs in the North East requiring continued humanitarian support.
